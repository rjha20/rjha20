Featured Products

1) House Blend Coffee


House blend coffee is a type of coffee that is made by blending different types of beans together. The exact ingredients of house blend coffee can vary from brand to brand, but typically, it includes a mixture of beans from different regions. This helps to create a coffee with a unique flavor profile that is different from other types of coffee. House blend coffee is often made with beans that have been roasted to medium or dark roast levels. This gives the coffee a bolder flavor that is well-suited for those who enjoy a stronger cup of coffee. If you’re looking for a coffee that is full of flavor and perfect for your morning cup, house blend coffee is a great option.

2) French Roast


French roast coffee beans offer a distinct charcoal-like flavour that some find sweet and smoky. It's much darker than other light roast coffees, so the fundamental flavours of each bean are often disguised by the taste of its roast instead. As such, regional characteristics within different types of coffee beans tend to be hidden due to this strong dark roast flavour overpowering them.
